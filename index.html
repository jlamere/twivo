twivo

Tivo for Twitter


Twivo is a hack made by Jennie Lamere. Twivo blocks the spoilers from your favorite TV shows on your Twitter timeline. Twivo then saves the tweets until your ready to watch the show, at which point the tweets will be displayed in real time. Twivo is an ultra light extension for Google Chrome. You can reach Jennie via Twitter or Email .


Download


Download twivo at the Google Chrome Store.

Record


Enter the TV shows for twivo to hide from your feed.

Play


Watch the tweets stream down in chronological order as you catch up on your TV.
